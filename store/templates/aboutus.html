About Kudumbashree

A movement to empower women and eradicate poverty, one neighborhood at a time.

Our Story

Kudumbashree is the poverty eradication and women empowerment programme implemented by the State Poverty Eradication Mission (SPEM) of the Government of Kerala. The name Kudumbashree in Malayalam means 'prosperity of the family'.

Our mission is to encourage women to engage in micro-enterprises, creating a supportive network where they can grow their skills, achieve financial independence, and lead their families to a better future. This online store is a platform to bring their hard work and incredible products to a wider audience.

Our Core Values

Empower Women

Providing financial independence and leadership opportunities.

Promote Local

Supporting local economies and traditional craftsmanship.

Ensure Quality

Delivering authentic, high-quality handmade products.

Sustainable Living

Creating livelihoods that are both profitable and eco-friendly.
